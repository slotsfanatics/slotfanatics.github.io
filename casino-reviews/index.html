Introduction to Casino Reviews

There are hundreds upon hundreds of online casinos in the online gaming market, so it is important for players to know which sites are worth registering with.

Game Selection

The best online casinos are those that offer up a wide range of online slots. It's important that you sign up at a site that provides players with hundreds of games so that there is always something new to try.

There are some online casinos like LeoVegas that provide players with a selection of games from a wide range of developers like Net Entertainment, Betsoft and NetGen. Many operators provide an exclusive collection of games; for example, Spin Palace provides mostly Microgaming slots while 888 has its own proprietary software. Both of these are great options; it all depends on what your tastes are.

Casino Bonuses

Our favourite online casinos are those that provide players with a wide range of bonuses and promotions. This encourages players stay interested by providing them with the chance to earn bonus cash, luxury prizes and free spins. There are several types of casino bonuses available, including:

– Free Spins: offer players a number of free spins on a particular title
– Deposit Bonus: players receive extra cash on top of their bonus
– Leaderboard Promotions: players earn points for playing certain games. The players at the top of the leaderboard receive cash and luxury prizes.
– Prize Draws: players earn tickets every time they wager a certain amount of money. The winners that are drawn receive cash prizes and luxury prizes.

Mobile Support

We aim to recommend online casinos that are available on both mobile and desktop devices. These operators provide players with the opportunity to play online slots from smartphones, tablets and personal computers. At these online casinos, players only need one account in order to access the games from multiple devices. Gaming wallets are also shared, so there is no need to deposit twice.

One of the great things about multi-platform casinos is that they offer up two different sets of bonuses for desktop and mobile devices. In many cases, players can earn double the points in leadboard promotions when they play on mobile platforms.

Mobile online casinos are usually browser-based while desktop casinos can be downloaded. However, more and more online casinos are opting to offer browser-based gaming which allows players quick and convenient access to their favourite games.

Customer Support

Online casinos are only as good as their customer service. So, at Slotsfanatics, it is important that the casinos we recommend take care of their players. We want to ensure that players can get in contact with customer support easily, by email, live chat and telephone. Another thing that we look out for is the inclusion of many different banking options, including e-wallets, credit cards, bank transfers and prepaid cards.
