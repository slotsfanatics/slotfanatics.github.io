Monster Mash
Kaiju means ‘strange beast’ in Japanese, and over time has become a generic term for giant monsters in fiction, including films and television shows about giant monsters. Most of the kaiju have been in multiple films or shows and are depicted as being either hostile towards or indifferent to humanity, depending on which iteration.

The best-known kaiju franchise or creation for most Western and non-Japanese consumers is the giant lizard Godzilla. Soon after its first appearance Godzilla would inspire a number of similar monster movie stars including Mothra, a giant moth and King Ghidora, a three-headed dragon from space. The formula soon proved so popular in Japan that Godzilla was joined by western cultural breakout King Kong in a crossover film.

Originally Godzilla, which in most early iterations was triggered by nuclear radiation, was a direct result of the bombings of Hiroshima and Nagasaki, events that took place less than a decade before the first film was released. Similarly, themes relating to the negative consequences of nuclear power and weaponry feature heavily in Mothra’s original outing. Nonetheless, as Godzilla and other kaiju films become more popular internationally and as time went by, the concepts and monster origins became either more ambiguous or featured more generalised pro-environmental themes.

Creature Features
This is a five reel pokie with a maximum of 20 possible paylines to bet on. Prizes are awarded for three, four and five-of-a-kind matches. These factors, combined with free spins, a scatter bonus round and two types of wild bonus make for a pokie with lots of chances to win big.

The maximum reward for the wild symbol is 100 coins, and it can also be used to substitute any normal symbol in a winning configuration. But if you find an exploding wild, not only will it count towards a regular wild match or substitution, but there is a chance that it will randomly turn two to five normal symbols into wild symbols too!

Finding three free spin scatter symbols will award you with five free spins, and successive matches can win you more, potentially giving you many chances to win. If you manage to find three bonus scatter symbols, the game will begin the special rampage bonus round.

With its stylish graphics and wide range of prizes, this is one pokie that’s sure to have mass appeal!
