History
Amaya Gaming has been around in the online gambling market for quite some time, but recently put its name on the map in 2014 when it acquired Rational Group (which operates Full Tilt Poker and Poker Stars) for $4.9 billion. At first, Amaya Gaming ran these two leading sites as poker-only platforms but eventually decided to branch out.
