Santa Paws is another great game from Microgaming that is available to play now on browsers and mobile/tablet devices. This features a winning combination of classic gameplay and modern functionality that is sure to appeal to all fans of free online slots.

Main Game

This game is made up of five reels with three rows each, laid out in a 5×3 configuration. There are 20 paylines that can be selected in series. Prizes are awarded to players for successful two, three, four and five-of-a-kind symbol matches on active paylines.

Prizes

The most valuable regular symbol is the postman wolf, awarding top prizes of 750 coins. Next is the seal holding presents, which is worth a maximum of 500 coins. Way down on the other end of the scale is the lowly 10 symbol, which awards a tiny top prize of 100 coins. Nevertheless, with numerous chances to win and features like multipliers, even these little amounts can soon add up.

Bonus Symbols

Santa Paws himself is the main substitute symbol, in addition to awarding massive prizes of up to 4,000 coins. This can be used in place of any regular symbol as part of a winning combination on an active payline. During free spins, he’s replaced by his mate Rudolph, who is functionally identical.

The second special symbol is the penguin. This is a scatter, which in this game means that it awards prizes and free spins for appearing in any configuration regardless of selected paylines. Finding three or more scattered on the reels will unlock 12 free spins alongside a corresponding multiplier, up to a maximum of 7x.
