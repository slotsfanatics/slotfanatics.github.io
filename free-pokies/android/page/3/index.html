Android
Android devices offer players the chance to play their favourite slots while on the go. There are many affordable options that allow players to access the best online casinos without having to be tied to a personal computer.

Benefits of Playing Slots on Android Devices
The main reason you would want to play slots from your Android is that you are not limited to visiting your favourite casinos only while you’re at home and on your personal computer. With Android smartphones and tablets, you can take your favourite mobile slots with you on the go. You can access hundreds of great games from a wide range of developers who offer up the most innovative mobile technology.

Another great feature of the Android operating system is that it offers up a wide range of choice. While Apple only provides players with a few choices for smartphones and tablets, there are dozens of options for Android devices. So, you can pick out a device that best suits your budget and personal gaming preferences.

HTML5 and Flash Technology
Normally, Android devices have the same restrictions as iPhones and iPads when it comes to playing Flash games. Normally, you wouldn’t be able to play Flash-based slots on your Android tablet or smartphones – but, there are some ways to get around this. You can download Android browsers like Skyfire (which have Flash capabilities) or download the Legacy version of Flash Player for Android. As such, Android users could potentially have a wider range of choice when it comes to playing pokies.

Still, Android users can access the wide range of HTML5 slots that are on the market. These are games developed with a universal programming language that can be accessed from all sorts of iOS, Windows and Android devices.

What Are the Best Android Devices for Playing Mobile Pokies?

  • Samsung Galaxy S7 Edge: this is one of the newest Android smartphones on the network. With a 5.5” screen and 4GB of RAM, it ensures that players will have a high-performance online gaming experience.
  • Nexus 6p: this is a smartphone that nearly crosses the line into being a tablet as it has a 5.7” display. So, if graphics are your main focus when it comes to mobile gaming, then this is the perfect Android smartphone for you. However, with just 2GB of RAM, the device may suffer from a bit of lag.
  • Google Pixel C: weighing 517g and measuring 242mm x 179mm x 7mm, this is a tablet that you can comfortably take with you anywhere. It has 10 hours of battery life, so you can play your favourite slots for hours on end, and the 3GB of RAM ensure a smooth gaming experience.
  • Nexus 9: weighing 270g and measuring 213.4mm x 123.6mm x 6.4mm, this is another ultra-portable tablet that offers up 3GB of RAM for high performance when it comes to online gaming.
  • Microsoft Surface Pro: with up to 16GB of RAM, this is one of the most powerful tablets on the market. If you want to use your tablet for gaming as well as other purposes, then this is the way to go.
