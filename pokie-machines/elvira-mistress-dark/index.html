Elvira: Mistress of the Dark

Elvira is one of the most iconic horror characters in history, combining sexiness and sassiness with classic horror themes. She's been around for decades and shows no signs of heading back to the underworld any time soon, so we-re happy that Aristocrat has honoured her with her very own poker machine, Elvira: Mistress of the Dark.

Unfortunately, Elvira: Mistress of the Dark is not yet available as an online casino game. However, you play plenty of other fantastic horror-themed free slots here, including Dracula, The Invisible Man and Frankenstein.

The Original Gothic Bad Girl

Elvira is a character brought to life by Cassandra Peterson, who first appeared as Elvira in a revamp of The Vampira Show. Throughout the 1960s and 1970s, Elvira went from a cult character to a full-blown brand and eventually went on to star in her own films. By the 1980s and 1990s, she had her own comic books, trading cards, action figures, perfume and calendars. Even today, she still makes television appearances and continues to be a popular figure in the horror genre.

Usually, Aristocrat bases its branded poker machines on hit television series and modern blockbuster movies - but the company has gone a different route with this game. It is great to see Aristocrat having some fun with a bit of weird theme with quirky humour and a cult following.

Format

Aristocrat's Elvira poker machine is a game that should appeal to all types of players. This is because it is a penny slots game that allows players to wager just 1c per spin. Both high rollers and casual gamers should get a kick out of this game - but, we can see why players with bigger budgets would want to spin the reels with a bit of a higher limit.
